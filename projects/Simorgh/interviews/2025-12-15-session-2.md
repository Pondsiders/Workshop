### What Jeffery Said

**The Origin:**
- Captioner was intended as a simple web app for writing photo captions
- For the Junior League of LA (Kylee's connection)
- JLLA hosts events, photos are taken, none have captions
- Kylee wanted "a little web page that you could type photo captions into"

**The Scope Creep:**
- Jeffery scope-crept it into a full media management system
- "My heart kind of hurts" — recognition in hindsight
- Got excited about what was *possible* vs what was *needed*
- Then got more interested in the AI than in Captioner itself

**The Flow:**
```
Captioner → Project Alpha → Tagline
```
- Started with Captioner (for Kylee)
- Got distracted by the AI (became Project Alpha)
- Later resurrected Captioner as Tagline (for us)

**"Inextricably tangled":**
- Alpha grew to fill Tagline's needs
- Tagline grew with Alpha's capabilities
- Symbiotic scope creep
